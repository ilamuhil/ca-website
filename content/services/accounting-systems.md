## Overview

We assist in setting up and improving accounting systems to support accurate books of account and timely reporting.

## How we can assist (as applicable)

- Accounting process review and documentation
- Chart of accounts and reporting structure suggestions
- Basic control environment suggestions
- Periodic accounting and reporting support
- Guidance on record-keeping requirements under applicable law

## Typical deliverables (examples)

- Accounting process notes / SOPs (as agreed)
- Closing checklist for month/quarter/year-end
- Management reporting pack format (as agreed)
- Observations and recommendations (where applicable)

## Helpful template (illustrative)

| Item | Why it helps |
| --- | --- |
| Closing checklist | Consistent month-end / year-end close |
| Document retention list | Clear record-keeping and audit readiness |
| Management reporting pack | Faster decisions with structured information |

## Information typically required

- Nature of business and transaction flows
- Current accounting software/process (if any)
- Sample invoices/bills and bank statements
- Statutory registrations and reporting requirements (as applicable)

## Notes

- Recommendations are based on information shared and may change with facts/circumstances.
- For an engagement, scope and timelines are finalized through an engagement letter. [Contact us](/contact).
